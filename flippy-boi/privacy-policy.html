1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Flippy Boi ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile game application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect or transmit any personal data to our servers. All game data (scores, coins, preferences, progress) is stored locally on your device and is never sent to us.

2.1 Third-Party Data Collection (Google AdMob)

While we do not collect your data, our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect information when ads are displayed, including:

  • Device Identifiers: Unique device identifiers and advertising IDs
  • Location Data: Approximate location data (if permitted by your device settings)
  • Usage Patterns: Information about how you interact with ads
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and technical specifications

This data collection is handled entirely by Google AdMob, not by us. We do not have access to or control over the data that AdMob collects. For more information about how Google AdMob uses data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect or receive any personal data from you, we do not use any personal information. All game data (scores, coins, preferences) is processed locally on your device only.

Third-Party Data Use (Google AdMob): Google AdMob uses the data it collects (as described in section 2.1) to:

  • Display relevant advertisements in the App
  • Personalize ad content (if you have consented to personalized ads)
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Comply with legal obligations

For details on how Google uses this data, visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

Our App uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier and collect information about your device and usage patterns. For more information about how Google uses data, visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's advertising settings or through the consent options provided in the App.

5. Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device only. We store your game data (scores, coins, preferences, progress) locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. This data is never transmitted to our servers - we do not have any servers that receive or store your data.

Third-Party Data Storage (Google AdMob): Data collected by Google AdMob is stored and managed by Google according to their privacy policy. We do not have access to or control over this data.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have certain data protection rights:

Regarding Data We Collect: Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete. All game data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

Regarding Data Collected by Google AdMob: For data collected by Google AdMob, you can:

  • Opt Out of Personalized Ads: Use the consent options in the App or adjust your device's advertising settings
  • Access AdMob Data: Contact Google directly or visit Google's Data & Privacy settings
  • Withdraw Consent: Change your ad personalization preferences in the App's Privacy Settings at any time

If you have questions about your rights or this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Data Security

Local Data Storage: Since all game data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security measures. We use secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system to protect your game data.

Third-Party Data Security (Google AdMob): Google AdMob implements security measures to protect the data it collects. For information about Google's data security practices, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
